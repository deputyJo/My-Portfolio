Recipe Collection

The Recipe Collection Site is a platform for food enthusiasts to explore, share, and save their favorite recipes. Users can search for recipes by category or ingredient, submit their own recipes, and create a personalized collection of their favorite dishes. The server-side logic is handled by Node.js and Express.js, providing a robust and scalable backend, while EJS is used for templating to dynamically render pages based on user interactions.
